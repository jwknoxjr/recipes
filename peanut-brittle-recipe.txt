Combine sugar, corn syrup together in a large bowl, stirring in 2 cups peanuts.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes.
Stir, then stir in 2 more cups peanuts.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes.
Stir again.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes.
Quickly remove from microwave, stir in baking powder, butter and cinnamon.
Pour out onto greased cookie sheet.
Let cool.
Break into decent sized pieces.
